# Privacy Policy for NourishNet

**Last Updated:** April 29, 2026

## Introduction

NourishNet ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and profile information when you sign up
- **Location Data**: Your address for food donation/pickup coordination (only when you provide it)
- **Profile Photos**: Images you upload for your profile or food listings

### Permissions We Request

- **Camera Permission**: Used only when you choose to take photos for your profile or food listings. We do not access your camera without your explicit action.
- **Photo Library Permission**: Used only when you choose to upload photos from your device.
- **Location Permission**: Used only when you choose "Use current location" to fill in your address.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Facilitate food donation and pickup coordination
- Display your profile and listings to other users
- Improve our services and user experience
- Communicate with you about your account and activities

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is securely stored using industry-standard encryption
- We use Supabase for authentication and data storage
- Photos are stored securely in cloud storage
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of location services
- Control what photos you share

## Camera and Photo Usage

- Camera access is only activated when you explicitly choose to take a photo
- We do not access your camera or photos in the background
- All photos are only uploaded when you confirm the action
- You can delete your uploaded photos at any time

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: For authentication and database services
- **Google Maps**: For address autocomplete and location services
- **Google Sign-In**: For authentication (optional)
- **Apple Sign-In**: For authentication on iOS (optional)

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
